# PropertyGraph — One-Pager

---

## What Is It?

PropertyGraph reconstructs ownership intelligence from fragmented real estate records.

Input: County deeds + LLC filings (public data)
Process: COSMIC entity resolution (proprietary AI)
Output: True ownership layer + deal signals

---

## Why It Matters

Real estate is priced on "what is visible."

- **LoopNet:** 5% (listed properties)
- **CoStar:** 30% (public transactions)
- **Brokers:** 20-40% (relationships)
- **PropertyGraph:** 100% (all ownership structures)

Whoever sees the entity chains first wins.

---

## For Institutional Investors

**Problem:** 
- Missing off-market deals (35% of deals don't hit LoopNet)
- Blind spots on competitor moves (PE firms assembling quietly)
- Incomplete due diligence (true ownership structures hidden)

**Solution:**
- Find deals 6 months before market
- See competitor portfolios assembling
- Reconstruct entity chains for risk assessment

**Result:**
3-5 additional off-market deals/year = $50-100M deal pipeline improvement
