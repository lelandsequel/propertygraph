## PropertyGraph Solution

Three layers of intelligence:

### Layer 1: Ownership Reconstruction
**Problem:** One property shows "John Smith LLC" as owner. Who's really behind it?

**PropertyGraph:** Expands the LLC. Shows:
- Parent entities (John Smith Holdings → Smithfield Capital → Boston Properties)
- Sibling entities (6 other LLCs with same manager)
- Individual principals (reveals the humans behind the structures)

**Result:** True ownership visibility. Hidden portfolios become visible.

### Layer 2: Portfolio Assembly Detection
**Problem:** Someone's buying quietly. When do you notice?

**PropertyGraph:** Detects patterns:
- Same entity acquiring 5+ adjacent parcels in 12-18 months
- Fragmented ownership clustering in same ZIP code
- Cross-entity control structures (coordinated buying)

**Result:** See roll-ups forming before competitors do.

### Layer 3: Opportunity Surfacing
**Problem:** Where's the next deal?

**PropertyGraph:** Shows:
- Properties near known SMC holdings (adjacency plays)
- Ownership clusters similar to past acquisitions (pattern matching)
- Undervalued segments in prime markets

**Result:** Deal sourcing accelerated by 3-6 months.
